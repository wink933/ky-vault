### 四则运算

- 和、差的导数（微分）  
$$[u(x)\pm v(x)]'=u'(x)\pm v'(x),\quad d[u(x)\pm v(x)]=d[u(x)]\pm d[v(x)].$$

- 积的导数（微分）  
$$[u(x)v(x)]'=u'(x)v(x)+u(x)v'(x),\quad d[u(x)v(x)]=u(x)d[v(x)]+v(x)d[u(x)].$$

- 商的导数（微分）  
$$\left[\frac{u(x)}{v(x)}\right]'=\frac{u'(x)v(x)-u(x)v'(x)}{[v(x)]^{2}},\quad v(x)\ne 0,$$  
$$d\left[\frac{u(x)}{v(x)}\right]=\frac{v(x),d[u(x)]-u(x),d[v(x)]}{[v(x)]^{2}},\quad v(x)\ne 0.$$
