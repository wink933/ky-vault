
> [!question]+ 题干
> $$I=\lim_{x\to +\infty}\left(\sqrt[6]{x^6+x^5}-\sqrt[6]{x^6-x^5}\right)=\underline{\hspace{6cm}}.$$


> [!success]- 解题步骤
> 【分析】  
$$\sqrt[6]{x^6+x^5}-\sqrt[6]{x^6-x^5}=x\left(\sqrt[6]{1+\frac{1}{x}}-1\right)-x\left(\sqrt[6]{1-\frac{1}{x}}-1\right),$$
>
>$$\begin{aligned}  
I&=\lim_{x\to +\infty}x\left(\sqrt[6]{1+\frac{1}{x}}-1\right)-\lim_{x\to +\infty}x\left(\sqrt[6]{1-\frac{1}{x}}-1\right)\  
&=\lim_{x\to +\infty}\left(x\cdot \frac{1}{6}\cdot \frac{1}{x}\right)-\lim_{x\to +\infty}\left(x\cdot \frac{1}{6}\cdot\left(-\frac{1}{x}\right)\right)  
=\frac{1}{6}+\frac{1}{6}=\frac{1}{3}.  
\end{aligned}$$


> [!failure]- 错误原因
> 错误原因类似于错题2和错题3

> [!note]- 总结
> 等价无穷小也是可以根据泰勒公式推出，二者等价
